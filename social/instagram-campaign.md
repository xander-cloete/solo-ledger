# Instagram Campaign — Solo Ledger

**Goal:** make people *feel* the app. Instagram is a visual feed — the hook is the
image, not the words. Solo Ledger's superpower here is that it's genuinely beautiful,
so lead with the 14 themes.

**Voice:** warm, casual, a little playful. First person. Short lines, tasteful emoji.
You're a student building something cool, not a brand.

**Cadence:** ~7 pieces over ~2 weeks. Mix formats — Reels get the most new reach,
carousels get saves, Stories keep you visible daily.

**Hashtags:** IG tolerates more (use 8–15). Mix sizes so you're not lost in huge tags.
Core set to remix:
`#buildinpublic #codinglife #webdev #reactjs #indiedev #uidesign #frontend
#personalfinance #fintech #studentdeveloper #appdesign #madewithcode #pwa #designinspo`

**Format tips:**
- IG strips clickable links from captions → say **"link in bio"** and put `[LIVE LINK]`
  in your bio (or a Linktree).
- Reels: first 1 second must show the app moving (theme switch). No slow intros.
- Carousels: slide 1 is a scroll-stopper; the last slide is your CTA.
- Post Reels/carousels 11am–1pm or 7–9pm; Stories anytime.
